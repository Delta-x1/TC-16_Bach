SS Debuts New Toronto City Collection

21:30, 22nd Febuary 2025

Author: Nguyen Viet Bach

The collection features collaborations with the Toronto Maple Leafs Hockey Team alongside with many others

AT 8:00 A.M on the 21st of Febuary, 2025, SS Reveals the hint of a new collaborated collection highlighting the Toronto Heritage that will be released the day after. In the evening of the same day, hundreds of people rushed into SS stores worldwide queuing for the newly released items. Today, just as SS stores worldwide opens at 8:00 A.M, people flooded into SS stores craving for the Toronto items.

SS also partners with the University of Toronto to create a Toronto exclusive merch that will only drop in the SS Toronto shop

This collection features jerseys from the Toronto Raptors and the Toronto Maple Leafs, paying tribute to the rich sports heritage of the city. The collection highlights a contrast between the color red and blue of the 2 sport teams, but still mixing it with grey or white signaling brotherhood and pride.

The items soon sold out just after a mere 30 minutes. Currently, resellers are selling these items from $1000 to $5900 on resell markets, quickly turning into the new hype.
